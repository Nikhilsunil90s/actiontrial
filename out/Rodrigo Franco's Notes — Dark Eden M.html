Dark Eden M

  • This page is a draft and pretty much a work in progress. Expect things to change a lot.

  • After playing MIR4 for half of 2021, I decided to start playing another korean MMO remake on mobile, Dark Eden M.

  • I remember fondly my 14 year old self playing the original Dark Eden in my crappy PC, and have been expecting the new release for a long time.

  • The game is heavily based on Diablo 2-era aesthetics. It was characterized by dark and gritty aesthetics, with a focus on dungeon crawling and loot acquisition. The gameplay loop consisted of delving into increasingly difficult dungeons in search of better loot, and using that loot to survive and defeat even tougher enemies. This loop was addictive and kept players coming back for more, as they strived to amass the best gear possible.
